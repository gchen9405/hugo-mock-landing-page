Virtual Try-On Technology 🛡️

Upload a photo and watch AI-powered fitting technology overlay outfits onto your body, ensuring you find the perfect fit and style—before you buy. FitPix analyzes your body shape and suggests the best size/items across different brands, reducing returns and making online shopping stress-free.

Mix & Match Outfits 🚀

Try on multiple items at once—combine tops, bottoms, shoes, and accessories from different brands to build complete outfits and see how they work together. Use your phone’s camera to see outfits in live AR mode, moving naturally with you for an even more realistic styling experience.

Personalized Recommendations 📈

Based on body shape, personal preferences, past try-ons, trending styles, price range, and comptability with items bought in the past, FitPix recommends new pieces tailored to your unique taste. FitPix additionally highlights small businesses that users may not find on their own.

Social Sharing & Feedback 🦉

Get opinions before you buy! Share your virtual outfits with friends, create polls, and get feedback to make confident fashion choices.

It Starts With a Swipe ✨

Bored and unsatisfied? FitPix allows you to swipe through a personally curated collection of tops, bottoms, and more (projected onto your body via AR technology) to efficiently see a high volume of outfits and how they look on you—sorta like Tinder!

Exclusive Deals and Alerts 🧰

Never miss a sale—Save your favorite outfits and get instant notifications when they go on sale or restock in your size, so you never miss out on the perfect piece at the best price.
